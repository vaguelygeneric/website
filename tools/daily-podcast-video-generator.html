How to use: Fill in your episode info, drop in your logo and audio file, then hit Preview animation to see a live preview. When you're happy, click Record & export video — it will play through the full episode and save a .webm video file you can upload to YouTube. Recording happens in real-time (same duration as your episode).

Tip: For future episodes, just change the episode number and swap the audio file. The template reuses everything else automatically.
